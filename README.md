# HR Management System (HRMS)

## Pages & Features

### 1. Dashboard
- Headcount summary (total, new hires, departures)
- Leave requests pending approval
- Upcoming birthdays & work anniversaries
- Department distribution chart

### 2. Employee Directory
- Searchable employee list with department, role, status filters
- Employee profile: personal info, job details, documents, emergency contacts
- Add / edit / deactivate employees
- Org chart visualization
- Employee photo & avatar

### 3. Attendance & Time
- Clock in / clock out (with geolocation optional)
- Monthly attendance calendar view per employee
- Late arrivals & absences summary
- Overtime tracking
- Timesheet approval workflow

### 4. Leave Management
- Apply for leave (type, dates, reason)
- Leave balance overview (annual, sick, personal)
- Manager approval / rejection with comments
- Leave calendar (team view)
- Leave policy configuration

### 5. Payroll
- Salary structure setup (base, allowances, deductions)
- Monthly payroll processing
- Payslip generation & distribution
- Tax calculation & summary
- Payroll history

### 6. Recruitment
- Job posting creation & publishing
- Applicant tracking pipeline (applied → screening → interview → offer)
- Candidate profile with resume & notes
- Interview scheduling
- Offer letter generation

### 7. Performance
- Goal setting & tracking per employee
- Performance review cycles (self, manager, 360°)
- Rating & feedback forms
- Performance history timeline

### 8. Documents
- Company-wide document repository
- Employee-specific documents (contracts, certifications)
- Document expiry alerts
- E-signature for acknowledgments

### 9. Reports
- Headcount trends, attrition rate
- Leave utilization, attendance summary
- Payroll cost breakdown
- Diversity & demographics

### 10. Settings
- Company profile & departments
- Leave types & policies
- Payroll configuration
- Roles & permissions
- Notification preferences
